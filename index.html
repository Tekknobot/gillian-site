About

Editorial precision shaped by decades in broadcast and publishing.

Gillian Taylor is an experienced caption editor and editorial professional whose work spans Canadian broadcast media, publishing, and accessibility services. Her career includes senior captioning and transcription roles with Bell Media, Corus Entertainment, and Alliance Atlantis, where accuracy, timing, and clarity were essential to delivering accessible television content to wide audiences.

With formal training in English, publishing, and court reporting, Gillian brings a disciplined editorial approach to language—balancing precision, readability, and tone. Her background includes manuscript editing, broadcast captioning, viewer communications, and transcription work supporting both media production and publishing environments.

Today, Gillian continues freelance editorial and captioning work while maintaining ongoing professional development in stenographic speedbuilding and transcription technologies.
